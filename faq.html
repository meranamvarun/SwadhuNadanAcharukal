How long does homemade Kerala pickle last?

When stored in a clean, dry, airtight jar and handled with a dry spoon, homemade Kerala pickle usually stays good for around 3 to 6 months. Refrigeration can help preserve freshness for longer.

Is fish pickle available in Kerala?

Yes. Fish pickle is one of our most loved Kerala acharukal, prepared in small batches using fresh fish, roasted spices, and traditional homemade methods.
