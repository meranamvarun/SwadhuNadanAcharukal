About Our Gooseberry Pickle Kerala Homemade

Our gooseberry pickle Kerala homemade recipe brings together the signature tartness of amla with Kerala-style roasted spice and oil seasoning for a balanced, deeply traditional achar. Gooseberry has a unique taste that starts sharp and develops into mild sweetness, which makes it perfect for pickle when handled correctly. We steam or soften the fruit carefully, remove moisture, and blend it with chilli, mustard, fenugreek, ginger-garlic, curry leaf, and oil to create a full-bodied masala coating. The result is tangy, warm, and satisfying without feeling heavy. It pairs very well with rice, curd rice, kanji, dosa, and chapati, making it a practical daily-use pickle for many households.

Gooseberry pickle FAQs

What sizes and prices are available for the gooseberry pickle?

Two bottle sizes are available: 250g for ₹50 and 500g for ₹100. You can order 1 to 5 bottles at a time directly on WhatsApp.

Is gooseberry pickle the same as amla pickle?

Yes. Gooseberry is known as amla in Hindi and nellikka in Malayalam — this is a traditional Kerala amla achar with layered tang and medium spice.

How long does the gooseberry pickle last?

Up to 6 months unopened. After opening, consume within 45 days for best taste, store in a cool dry place, and always use a clean, dry spoon.
